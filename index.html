About EcoNova

EcoNova is a gamified learning platform designed to make environmental education engaging and fun for students of all ages. Our mission is to foster a deeper understanding and appreciation of our natural world through interactive games and activities.

Teachers can track student progress, assign activities, and customize learning paths based on individual needs and interests.

Make a Real-World Impact

At EcoNova, we believe that environmental education should extend beyond the classroom. Our platform combines online learning with real-world activities that make a tangible difference in your community.

Complete challenges like planting trees, organizing clean-ups, or reducing waste at home. Upload photos of your completed tasks to earn special badges and recognition from your teachers and peers.
